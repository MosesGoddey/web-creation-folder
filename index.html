About us

our story

Classic Restaurant was founded in 2022 in the heart of Old Airport, Jos, with a passion to celebrate the rich and diverse flavors of African cuisine. What started as a humble idea has grown into a favorite spot for families, food lovers, and visitors looking for authentic African dishes served with love. Every meal we prepare is a reflection of our culture, tradition, and hospitality because here, food is more than a dish, it’s a story.

our philosophy

At Classic Restaurant, we believe food should bring comfort, joy, and connection. We use only the freshest local ingredients to create traditional African meals that honor the flavors passed down through generations. Our goal is not just to serve you, but to give you a classic, home-cooked experience one plate at a time, our priority is reaching customers satisfaction, and making sure they dine a food that taste better

meet the team

Our team is a blend of culinary experts and passionate food lovers who share a common goal, to create unforgettable dining experiences. From our chefs who craft each dish with care, to our friendly staff who ensure you feel at home, we are dedicated to making your visit special. Our strength lies in our team passionate chefs, warm-hearted servers, and dedicated staff who bring Classic Restaurant to life every day.
